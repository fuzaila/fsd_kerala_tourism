KOTTAYAM

Previous Next

Kottayam is a marvelous coastal town of Kerala that is located at a distance of 60 km from Kochi. Not only is the city famous for the mesmerizing view of the backwaters, but it is a trading hub as well. Many tourists leave out Kottayam while planning there Kerala trip, but they do not know what they are missing.

The town is quite close to some of the most picturesque sight-seeing spots in India, including Aruvikkuzhi waterfalls, Elaveezha Poonchira, Kumarakom, and many more. This city also boasts of some of the most famous temples of Kerala, namely Vaikom temple, Bharananganam, and many more. Kottayam is also the home to the mesmerizing Vembanad lake that is famous all around the globe. If you visit during certain months of the upcoming year, you will be able to take part in exciting activities like viewing the boat race, relish delicious dishes in the annual food festival, and many more.

Kottayam, a town situated in south-central Kerala is an epitome of beauty between Kerala hill country and Vembanad. Popularly known as a hub of trade and commerce, the district is an eminent destination for literature and education. Situated amidst the beauty of nature, Kottayam has a lot to offer to its tourists. Offering breathtaking beauty with its majestic hills and scenic valleys, the district is largely visited by nature lovers. In addition to the surrounding beauty, Kottayam also offers a number of waterfalls that accentuates the beauty of the southern town. The huge spray of water droplets fills the surrounding air with extravagant scenic beauty and a positive vibe.


MAJOR THINGS TO DO

  1. Enjoy The Secluded Beauty

    If you are looking for a place where you want peace, secludedness, and calm, you have to visit Vaikom. The simple beauty it offers is irresistible and to see the most beautiful backwaters is simply mesmerizing. If you are looking for some alone time for yourself, then Vaikom is the right place for you to spend your holiday. You have the Vaikom Beach, the Vaikom temple to visit if you are making the most out of this place.

    Apt for: Solo travelers, couples, family too.
    Location: Kottayam, Kerala

  2. Experience The Beauty Of Nature

    Elveezha Poonchira is surrounded by three hills- Mankunnu, Kodayathoormala, Thonippara. If you are making your way to Kottayam, you cannot miss this place and it is a must-see. You will love its magnificent views and lush greenery and this has the most beautiful waterfalls and alcoves. Elveezha Poonchira is a perfect picnic spot so if you happen to stay nearby, you can grab some packed lunch and come here to have a picnic. This is not even an hour away from Kottayam City.

    Price: Free
    Location: Elaveezha Poonchira, Kerala

  3. Gaze At The Beauty of Waterfalls

    When in Kottayam, you need to make sure to go to these dangerous and beautiful waterfalls. If you are looking for some great adventure, then challenge yourself to reach these waterfalls. You need to go in Jeeps as it is difficult to travel in those narrow paths. However, it is all worth the effort to reach this beauty. The calm and dangerous path to the waterfalls is what makes it all worth it!

    Apt for: Adventure seekers, friends.
    Location: Marmala Rd, Vellani, Erattupetta, Kerala

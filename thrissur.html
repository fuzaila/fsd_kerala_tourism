THRISSUR

Previous Next

Referred to as the cultural Capital of Kerala, famous for advanced traditional performing arts of Kerala, religious places and world-famous Thrissur Pooram and Onam festival, Thrissur is as beautiful as you could imagine or maybe more! Popular among pilgrims all over the world as three significant temples of Lord Shiva are located in Thrissur this one also is a holy city. Thrissur is an abbreviation of Thiru- Shiva- Per-Ur that signifies a city of three temples of God Shiva.

Thrissur is a place from where Kerala gets its charm of festivals. It was the capital of the Kingdom of Kochi, festivals here are celebrated in a very comprehensive manner. The celebrations of Thrissur are the major attractions for travellers, and your trip is incomplete if you will not witness the famous festivals of Thrissur. There are many tourists’ spots in Thrissur like Shakthan Thampuran Tomb, Archaeology Museum, Athirapally falls and more and naturally with those come stay options!


MAJOR THINGS TO DO

  1. Marvel At The Intricate Beauty

    No list of best places to visit in Thrissur can ever be complete without including the Shakthan Thampuran Palace in it. Built by Raja Rama Varma IC in 1791, the palace’s Dutch-style architecture is a captivating sight to behold, especially for art admirers. Though, what makes it unique are the attractions inside it like a small museum that showcases antiquities and old relics.

    Apt for: Solo travelers, couples, families.
    Location: Stadium Rd, Chembukkav, Thrissur

  2. Listen To The Water Gushing

    Located 60 km from the city, Athirapally Falls is amongst the top places to see in Thrissur. The 80 feet high and 330 feet wide falls come all the way from the Anamudi Mountains and are often called as the Niagara Falls of India. If you’re looking for a blissful escape, don’t think much and head right to this place.

    Time: 8am - 6pm
    Location: Athirappilly, Chalakudy Taluk, Thrissur

  3. Get Ready To Explore More

    If you’re looking for surreal places to visit in and around Thrissur, then Vilangankunnu is the ideal spot to be. This beautiful hillock is located 7 km away from the city and has been converted into an amusement park. While there are rides for kids in the park, the gorgeous views make it equally worth visiting for others.

    Location: Amala Nagar, Thrissur, Kerala

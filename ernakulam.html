ERNAKULAM

Previous Next

Tinted with the garish colours of history and magnificence of Kerala, Ernakulam is the most lively and stunning destination of God’s Own Country – Kerala. With hoards of shopping markets, multiplexes, amusement parks, industrial buildings, marine drive, and residential complexes, this ancient kingdom is known as the Commercial Capital of Kerala.

The Western Ghats and Forests surrounding this beautiful district boast of some stunning and spectacular waterfalls where you can get soaked in the beauty of nature. The glistering water making its way through the lush green surrounding and tumbling down the hills is an enchanting sight.

There are many beautiful waterfalls near Ernakulam that one should visit and explore. These amazing waterfalls will not only fill you with a new found energy, but also recharge you for when you get back to your daily routine. Often called the land where China and India collide, Kochi in Karnataka has always been the place which attracts traders, explorers and travelers from around the world. Resting on a magnificent estuary, this city in Kerala holds the heritage of over 600 years. And while there is a lot of things to do around here in Kochi, there is another gem which makes a perfect escape for a relaxing weekend.


MAJOR THINGS TO DO

  1. Explore The Past

    The Mattancherry Palace is locally famous as the Dutch Palace which showcases the beautiful architectural beauty of Kerala in the most unique way. Influenced by the colonial rule, this marvellous building is located at a distance of 12 Kms. The travelers can visit this place to witness the extravagant architectural beauty of this palace.

    Timings: 10AM-5PM (Friday closed)
    Nearest railway station: Ernakulam, about 10 kms

  2. Escape To Nature

    Take a break from the hustle-bustle of the city and head to PAniely Puru which is located by the bank of the River Periyar. This is one of the most beautiful spots to visit in Ernakulam and managed by the Kerala Forest Department. Adorned by the lush-greenery and small hills, this place offers an ultimate experience of calmness and bliss. At this place, one can see the Periyar flow from the East to West through Malayattoor.

    Location: Paniyeli Poru Road, Near, Kodanad, Kerala

  3. Enjoy A Day Amidst Wildlife

    A perfect abode for the bird lovers and nature enthusiasts, Thattekad Bird Sanctuary is Kerala’s first bird sanctuary. Established in 1983, this sanctuary is spread across an area of 25-sqm. This place is home to around 284 species of birds which rests close to the north of the Periyar River. Known for the abundant number of birds residing within the region, this is one of the best things to see in Ernakulam.

    Apt for: Nature lovers, peace seekers, photography enthusiasts.
    Location: Kothamangalam-Pooyamkutty Road, Nyayapally PO, Thattekad

GETTING THERE

Nearest airport: Cochin International Airport, Ernakulam- 27 km to the north of Kochi. Nearest port: Willingdon Island, Kochi
